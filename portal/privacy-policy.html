Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Vanguard Insurance Group ("we", "our", "us") operates the Vanguard Client Portal mobile application and web service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Vanguard Client Portal, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, phone number, and business details provided during account setup by your insurance agent.
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials (email and password) used to access the portal. Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plaintext.
  • Policy Information: Insurance policy details, certificate of insurance requests, and document access records associated with your account.
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage information such as login timestamps and feature usage for service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide secure access to your insurance account
  • Display your active policies, documents, and billing information
  • Process certificate of insurance (COI) requests and deliver them to specified recipients
  • Send password reset tokens and account-related notifications
  • Improve and maintain the reliability of our services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • COI Recipients: When you request a certificate of insurance, the certificate and related policy information are sent to the email addresses you specify.
  • Insurance Carriers: Policy information may be shared with your insurance carriers as part of normal insurance operations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • JWT-based authentication with token expiration
  • Rate limiting on authentication endpoints

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your insurance policies are active with us. If you wish to have your data removed, please contact your insurance agent or email us directly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you through the app's Profile section
  • Request correction of inaccurate information by contacting your agent
  • Request deletion of your account data

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through your assigned insurance agent or visit our website.
